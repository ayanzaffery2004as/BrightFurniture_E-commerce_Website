Warranty Support

We stand by the quality of our products with reliable warranty support.

Warranty Coverage

At Bright Furniture, we offer a 1-year warranty on manufacturing defects for all our furniture products.

  • Structural damage due to manufacturing faults
  • Defects in materials like wood, fabric, foam, or hardware
  • Issues with product assembly provided by Bright Furniture

What is Not Covered

Our warranty does not cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by misuse, accidents, or improper maintenance
  • Damage due to exposure to extreme heat, moisture, or chemicals
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications

How to Claim Warranty

To initiate a warranty claim:

  1. Contact us via phone at 8092543236 or email us (brightfurniture2008@gmail.com).
  2. Provide your purchase invoice, product photos, and a description of the issue.
  3. Our team will review your request and schedule an inspection or service if eligible.

Note: Warranty service is limited to the original delivery location.
